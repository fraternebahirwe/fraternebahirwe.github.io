About Me

I am a passionate junior front-end developer based in Kigali, Rwanda. I enjoy creating modern, responsive, and user-friendly websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My journey in web development started with a curiosity about how websites work, and it quickly became a passion. I am constantly learning new technologies and improving my skills through personal projects, coding challenges, and open-source contributions. I enjoy solving problems, building creative solutions, and turning ideas into real digital experiences. Recently, I have been contributing to open-source projects, collaborating with other developers, and gaining practical experience with Git and GitHub. My goal is to become a professional software developer and contribute to impact projects that help people and businesses. When I am not coding, I enjoy spending time outdoors, learning new things, and exploring opportunities to grow both personally and professionally.
